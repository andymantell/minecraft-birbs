# Sound Credits

All bird call recordings in this mod are sourced from [xeno-canto.org](https://xeno-canto.org) and are licensed under Creative Commons. Full attribution for each recording is below.

All recordings were converted from MP3 to mono 44.1kHz Vorbis .ogg:
```
ffmpeg -i input.mp3 -ss START -t DURATION -ac 1 -ar 44100 -c:a libvorbis -q:a 4 output.ogg
```
