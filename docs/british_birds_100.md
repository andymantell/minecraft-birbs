# 100 Most Recognisable British Bird Species

Compiled from RSPB Big Garden Birdwatch data, BTO Garden BirdWatch records, and standard ornithological references. Species are grouped by family/category and ordered by approximate familiarity within each group.

## Notes

- **Size** is approximate adult body length (beak tip to tail tip). For species with significant sexual dimorphism (e.g. Pheasant), a typical or average value is given.
- **Rankings** within each group reflect a rough order of how commonly encountered the species is, drawing on RSPB Big Garden Birdwatch 2025 data (top 10: House Sparrow, Blue Tit, Woodpigeon, Starling, Blackbird, Robin, Great Tit, Goldfinch, Magpie, Long-tailed Tit) and BTO Garden BirdWatch records.
- Scientific nomenclature follows the BOU British List (as of December 2025, 636 species).
